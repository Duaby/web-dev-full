How to Subscribe and Receive Recipes

Follow these steps to subscribe to the restaurant recipe service and receive recipes by email.

Prerequisites
Make sure the Django server runs. Start it with python manage.py runserver. Access the site at http://127.0.0.1:8000.

Steps to Subscribe

1. Open your web browser.
2. Go to the landing page at http://127.0.0.1:8000.
3. Find the subscription form on the page.
4. Enter your full name in the name field.
5. Enter your email address in the email field.
6. Click the submit button.
7. You see a success message on the page.

What Happens Next

The system saves your information as a subscriber.
It sends a welcome email to your address.
The email contains sample recipes.
Check your email inbox for the message.
If you do not see it, check your spam folder.

Troubleshooting

If the email does not arrive, verify your email address is correct.
Ensure the server has email settings configured.
Check the server logs for errors.
Try subscribing again with a different email if needed.

Contact Support

If issues persist, contact the site administrator.
